Player Screen Page















Instructions

You can add characters by typing in their name and clicking the '+' Button.

You'll be able to adjust their Current Health, Max Health, and add custom status conditions or notes.

After typing what you want to update, press 'enter', 'tab', or click away from the box for it to update.

You can remove a character by going to the 'Delete Character' section, selecting the character from the dropdown and clicking the 'Delete Character' button.

When you adjust anything on the DM screen, you should see an appropriate change on the Player Screen Page.

The character information is shared per browser and will go away on refresh.
